Permit Restrictions

  • In effect: 7:30AM to 4:30PM, Mon-Fri
  • On Weekends: Brown and Reserved Space restrictions remain in effect. All other permit restrictions, meters, and toll areas are lifted
  • On University Holidays: Gold, Silver, and Brown and reserved space restrictions remain in effect. All other permit restrictions, meters, and toll areas are lifted
  • No Classes: Green, Red, Park and Ride, “Any Permit”, and Motorcycle/Scooter restrictions are lifted
  • Other Areas Enforced At All Times: carpool zones, service drives, disabled spaces, loading zones, “no parking” areas, bike lanes, sidewalks, grass areas and reserved spaces
