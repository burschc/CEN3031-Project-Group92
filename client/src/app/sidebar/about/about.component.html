About

Hello! We are a software engineering team working to help students, staff, and visitors find their way around campus. For those unfamiliar with the current parking structure, it becomes inconvenient for them to find the next available parking spot. Few resources provide users with details on the parking situation and the places where they can park, thus making it more difficult for every driver trying to park on campus. To address this issue, we have added filtering options to the map such as defining the type of permit the user has, the time of day when certain parking spots are available, road closures, etc.
